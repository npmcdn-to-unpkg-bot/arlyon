Hello!

This tool was designed to fill in a hole that was missing. I couldn't find a modern catalog tool for keep track of my stuff, and really like seeing my life in terms of raw data. How many shirts do I own? How long does the average pair of jeans last? There are some apps out there, but they all are either a) for osx or b) have an old and complicated ui. So I decided to write my own, as I tend to do with a lot of other holes that need filling. Why am I being boring and throwing my conversion rate out the window? Because, to be honest, I'm not looking for mass distribution. If I help someone file an insurance claim great! Since this was designed for me, it is for you for free (assuming I dont suddenly become super popular). Here are some features I value.
